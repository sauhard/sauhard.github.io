QUICK GUIDE
(press h to open again later)

Click anywhere to start drawing.
Click on a cell to color it. Click and drag over multiple cells to color them all!
Alternatively, you can press spacebar to toggle drawing by just hovering.
You can toggle various settings from the menu on the right.
Feel free to play around with it; everything can be toggled back (EXCEPT CLEAR)!
